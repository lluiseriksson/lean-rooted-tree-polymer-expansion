We present a Lean 4 formalization of a finite target-preserving leaf-summation mechanism for a second Ursell expansion of a hard-core polymer gas with holes. The proof retains the exact target union until modified-metric decay has been extracted, then marks a root in the active skeleton and eliminates child polymers bottom-up through parent-normalized incompatibility kernels. A parent-profile argument bounds the normalized aggregate child-factorial weight of rooted labelled spanning trees by 4^n. If M is the geometric moment constant, the formalized marked-root estimate is (n+1) S_n(r) <= M(4M^2)^n, and the target-sensitive order term satisfies T_n(Y) <= M exp(-rho m(Y))(4M^2)^n. The artifact pins the exact Lean, Mathlib, and upstream source revisions and exposes stable publication-facing theorem names. The result is finite and combinatorial-geometric; it does not prove the model-specific Yang-Mills activity estimate, a continuum limit, or a mass gap.
