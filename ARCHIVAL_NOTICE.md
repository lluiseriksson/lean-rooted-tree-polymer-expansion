# Archival Notice

This repository is closed as a final reproducible proof artifact. The terminal
published release is `v2.4.3`.

The project should be read as a Lean 4 formalization and artifact-engineering
record for three finite rooted-tree/polymer-expansion endpoints. It is not
maintained as an active research paper claiming independent new mathematical
theory. In particular, no priority claim is made for the classical
tree-graph/combinatorial ingredients beyond the scoped machine-checked
assembly recorded here.

The repository does not prove a concrete Yang--Mills activity estimate,
`hRpoly`, a continuum construction, Osterwalder--Schrader reconstruction, or a
mass gap. Those boundaries are part of the final artifact and should not be
weakened in citations or downstream summaries.

The standalone v2.4.2 PDF/LaTeX export bundle is retained externally as a
historical typeset snapshot. The canonical source remains this repository's
integrated `docs/paper/` tree and the deterministic `v2.4.3` release evidence.

No further roadmap items are planned for this repository. Substantial future
mathematics should live in a new project or a separately versioned upstream
development, not as a silent expansion of this artifact.
