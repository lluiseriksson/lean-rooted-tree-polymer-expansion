Dear Editor,

Please consider **“Marked Rooted-Tree Summation for Polymer Cluster Expansions
with Holes: A Lean 4 Formalization.”** The work isolates and machine-checks a
finite target-preserving mechanism used in quantitative polymer expansions: an
exact target union is retained until modified-metric decay is extracted, after
which a marked-root, parent-normalized leaf recursion closes the remaining tree
sum with explicit ratio `4 M^2`.

The contribution is deliberately scoped. It is not presented as a proof of a
Yang--Mills mass gap or a continuum construction. The submission instead offers
an auditable formalization result with stable Lean endpoints, immutable source
pins, an axiom oracle, a theorem-to-paper map, and a documented clean-build
procedure.

The article is integrated into the repository documentation rather than tracked
as a separate PDF, preventing drift between exposition and artifact. An
archived release and CI record should be inserted into the final submission
once available.
