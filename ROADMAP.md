# Roadmap

The repository is closed at `v2.4.3`. No further roadmap items are planned for
this artifact.

The theorem interface, claims boundary, documentation model, release evidence,
and repository identity are intended to remain fixed. The project should be
cited or reused as an archival Lean 4 proof artifact, not as an active research
programme.

Substantial future work, including a concrete raw gauge activity estimate,
`hRpoly`, a sharper tree-profile theorem, a continuum construction, or a
different polymer geometry, should live in a new project or a separately
versioned upstream development. Such work is not part of the theorem claimed in
`v2.4.3`.
