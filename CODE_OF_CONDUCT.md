# Code of conduct

Participants should be respectful, precise, and constructive. Harassment,
personal attacks, discriminatory conduct, and deliberate disruption are not
acceptable.

Technical disagreement is welcome. Critique claims and proofs rather than
people; distinguish formal verification, mathematical interpretation, and
speculation; and disclose uncertainty when evidence is incomplete.

Maintainers may edit or remove abusive contributions and restrict participation
when necessary to keep the project safe and productive.
